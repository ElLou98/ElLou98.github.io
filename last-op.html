Last'OP

Academic project

In this game O.R.E is a robot that was programmed to activate after the extinction of the human race to restore it. Unfortunately, the earth is no longer home to any living species and as a result O.R.E was activated to accomplish its mission. He will then have to cross several biomes to recover the necessary modules to make the earth live again

Gitlab repository

Features that i worked on :

  • Levitating object
  • Outline interactable objects
  • Levitating the player
